INTRODUCING LA KI

We are a California & Mexico-based non-profit with the purpose of accelerating well-being for all.

Meet La Ki’s Co-Creators

FEELWELL

We have designed FeelWell as a new operating system with key technologies that support the acceleration and coordination of well-being at scale.

We are presently introducing FeelWell in Tulum, Mexico with community activities that connect the elements of physical, emotional and environmental wellness. Our events are intended to support Tulum’s local community, as well as those that are in Tulum from abroad, with the intention to support unity and help dissolve socioeconomic, cultural and language barriers.

@i_feelwell

KI CHAIN

KI Chain is an energy and resource supply chain and is an evolution of our present day supply and currency chain models, including Blockchain.

It is a regenerative supply chain system and serves to provide communities with nutrients, living spaces, supplies, power, transport, and human connection.

@la_ki_chain

SUNSKYA

SunSkya is La KI’s nine year global tour. We are traveling as a physical and digital collective through key global areas, starting in the Riviera Maya.

We invite you to join us in our first part of the tour we’re calling: Restore & Rewild in the Riviera Maya.

@sunskyatour
